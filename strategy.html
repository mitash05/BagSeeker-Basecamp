Protocol: The Liquidity Trap

THE "99% PROBABILITY" SETUP
Tracking the Market Maker's Footprints.

The Logic

Retail traders place their Stop Losses at obvious Highs and Lows. Market Makers (Whales) need liquidity to fill their large orders.

They push price past the level to trigger those stops (The Trap), collect the liquidity, and then immediately reverse.

WE DO NOT CHASE THE BREAKOUT. WE WAIT FOR THE MURDER.

01

Identify The Kill Zone

Look at the 15m or 1H chart. Find a clean "Previous High" or "Previous Low" that looks too obvious.

ACTION: Draw a horizontal line at that price level.

02

The Sweep (The Hunt)

Wait for price to break that line aggressively. Retail traders will scream "BREAKOUT!" and FOMO in.

⚠️ DO NOT ENTER YET. If price stays above the line, it is a real breakout. We need it to FAIL.

03

The Reclaim (The Trigger)

Wait for a candle to CLOSE back inside the range. This confirms the breakout was fake. The trap is sprung.

  • ENTRY: Immediately on the candle close.
  • STOP LOSS: Just above/below the wick of the sweep.
  • TARGET: Liquidity on the opposite side of the range.
